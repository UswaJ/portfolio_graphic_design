PERFORMANCE MARKETING

Vistamax Campaigns

Overview: Driving lead generation and organic traffic through SEO and Paid Ad strategies.

The Objective

To increase brand visibility and generate qualified leads for Vistamax Consultants over a 6-month period. The goal was to optimize ad spend (ROAS) while simultaneously building long-term organic traffic.

Strategic Execution

  • Meta Ads: Designed and tested A/B variations of creative assets, focusing on high-contrast visuals and direct-response copy.
  • SEO: Conducted keyword research to identify low-competition / high-intent search terms and optimized blog content to rank for them.
  • Content Calendar: Managed a consistent posting schedule to engage the audience across Instagram and LinkedIn.
