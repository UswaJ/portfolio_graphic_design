EDITORIAL DESIGN & MANAGEMENT

Comsight Magazine

Overview: Leading a team of 20+ creatives to produce the university's flagship publication.

The Challenge

The society needed to launch a 50-page publication within 4 weeks. The challenge was coordinating 20 contributors, ensuring brand consistency, and managing the print production pipeline without delays.

The Solution

I implemented an Adobe InDesign workflow using Master Pages and Paragraph Styles to automate formatting. I also established a strict "Review & Approval" pipeline using Trello to track the status of every article and illustration.
