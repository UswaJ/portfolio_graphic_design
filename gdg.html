BRAND MANAGEMENT & EVENTS

Google Developer Groups

Overview: Leading the graphics team to scale global branding for campus tech events.

The Challenge

As a global community, Google Developer Groups (GDG) has strict visual guidelines. The challenge was to create unique, high-energy marketing materials for local hackathons and workshops while remaining 100% compliant with Google's global brand standards.

The Solution

I developed a library of reusable Canva and Illustrator templates for the team, ensuring consistent typography (Google Sans) and color usage. I coordinated with the marketing head to deliver assets for social media, print banners, and ID badges under tight deadlines.
